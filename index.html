Roses are red... or are they?

There are over 150 species of roses. The most familiar are the red flowers, but they come in many colors. Some of the more common colors are pink, white and yellow, but the gorgeous flowers can dress in even darker colors as crimson and mulberry. Few of the roses species are mixed-colour.

Possible colors for roses:

  • Red
  • White
  • Yellow
  • Pink
  • Crimson
  • Mauve
  • Lilac
  • Mulberry
